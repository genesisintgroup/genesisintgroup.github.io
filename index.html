Property overview

A colourful corner of St. Philip, full of character.

Tucked into a quiet corner lot in Harmony Hall, this two-storey Bajan home pairs classic wall-house architecture with a wave of Caribbean colour — soft coral walls, a mint-green veranda, and a hand-finished scalloped parapet that gives the roofline real personality. A wraparound upper balcony with turned white balusters overlooks the covered ground-floor porch, while a private, walled front garden of hibiscus and ixora frames the entrance.

Inside, the two-level layout offers flexible living — space for a growing family on the main floor, with a separate balcony-accessed level upstairs that could suit guests, extended family, or rental income. A covered carport and generous paved driveway complete the property, all set behind a decorative block perimeter wall for privacy from the street.

Location

Harmony Hall, St. Philip

St. Philip is Barbados' largest parish, stretching across the island's breezy south-east — home to Grantley Adams International Airport, the dramatic Atlantic coastline, and some of the island's most laid-back south-coast beaches. Harmony Hall sits within easy reach of local shops, schools and the main routes connecting to Bridgetown and the south coast.
