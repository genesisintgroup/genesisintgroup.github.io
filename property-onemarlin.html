Overview

A turnkey studio in a boutique garden community

Set within the One on Marlin development in Cheshire Hall and Richmond Hill, Providenciales, this fully furnished 448 sq ft studio offers an open-concept kitchen, living and sleeping area finished in warm timber and stainless steel, opening onto a private balcony surrounded by lush native vegetation. The unit is one of 35 self-contained studios spread across 10 detached villas in Phase One of the development, with on-site parking available for residents and guests.

Cheshire Hall and Richmond Hill is one of the most central and established subdivisions on the island, served by the Leeward Highway with easy access to shopping, dining, and the international airport — making this an attractive option as a personal pied-à-terre, a long-term rental, or a vacation-rental investment.

Location

Cheshire Hall & Richmond Hill, Providenciales

Cheshire Hall and Richmond Hill is one of the most central and developed subdivisions in Providenciales, stretching from The Bight in the east to Downtown in the west, and served throughout by the Leeward Highway. The immediate neighbourhood includes Graceway IGA, Windsor Plaza, and a mix of residential, retail and light commercial development, with a predominantly middle-to-high income demographic and convenient access to the international airport.
