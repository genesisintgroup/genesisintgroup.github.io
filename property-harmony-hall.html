Overview

A bright, two-story home with room to grow

Set back from the road on a well-proportioned lot in Harmony Hall, St. Philip, this two-story home is finished in a cheerful coral-and-mint exterior with a covered carport and mature landscaping. The layout spans two full floors, offering flexible living space for a family, extended household, or a mix of living and rental use. The property sits on 5,423 sq ft of registered land, with an additional 2,084 sq ft of road reserve, for a total surveyed area of 7,507 sq ft.

This is a well-established residential pocket of St. Philip, within easy reach of Bridgetown, the south coast, and Grantley Adams International Airport.
